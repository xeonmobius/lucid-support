Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is my dream data private? All dreams are stored locally on your device. Cloud sync is optional and encrypted.
  • Does this work offline? Yes, the entire app works without an internet connection.
  • How do reality checks work? The app sends periodic reminders during the day to check if you're dreaming, building the habit of lucidity.
  • What is MILD? Mnemonic Induction of Lucid Dreams, a bedtime technique where you repeat a mantra to increase dream awareness.
